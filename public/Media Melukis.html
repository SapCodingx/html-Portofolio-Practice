THE BEST ART MEDIUMS ACCORDING TO SAPNAH

My top 3 mediums.

Watercolors

I love the transparencies of watercolor and how I can control the water.

Pencil

Pencis are slow medium but I enjoy using them and I can make fine details. Both pencil and colored pencils are my favorite.

Gouache

Gouache is a fun medium. They feel similar to watercolor but they are opaque and you can add thicker layers.
